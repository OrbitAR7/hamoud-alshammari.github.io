About Me

I completed a coursework-based M.S. in Aerospace Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, where I worked closely with faculty in orbit mechanics, geodesy, and remote sensing. My graduate training fostered a strong foundation in estimation theory, reference frames, and spacecraft dynamics, and it shaped my commitment to advancing precise and reliable space operations through research in orbit determination, dynamical systems, and control.

My research interests span the intersection of classical orbital dynamics and modern sensing technologies: orbit determination, spacecraft state estimation (MEKF), radar and LiDAR systems, geodetic reference frames, and (select) machine learning applications for geosciences. I’m pursuing a PhD to connect rigorous orbital dynamics with sensing for real-world space applications.

Selected Research Projects (Detailed)

TerraSAR-X Control

Replicating TerraSAR-X Orbit Control

Recreated TerraSAR-X’s 11-day sun-synchronous repeat orbit and control strategy. Built truth/reference orbits with J2 & drag, designed ΔV maneuvers to remain within a 250 m tolerance tube, and explored maneuver cadence vs. error growth and propellant.

Methods & Tools

Orbit Propagation Sun-Sync/Repeat Control Budget MATLAB/Simulink
Outcome: Demonstrated control feasibility under perturbations; outlined extensions for SRP and higher-fidelity drag.
MEKF Simulation

Closed-Loop ADCS with MEKF

Developed a comprehensive ADCS simulation: rigid-body attitude dynamics with environmental torques, Earth-pointing PD controller, reaction-control actuators with error models, multi-sensor attitude suite, and a six-state MEKF for attitude & gyro-bias estimation. Robustness evaluated via 100-run Monte Carlo.

Methods & Tools

MEKF PD Control Sensor Fusion Monte Carlo
Outcome: Bias convergence and pointing accuracy achieved across disturbance cases; sensitivity to sensor noise quantified.
SAR Processing

SAR Image Reconstruction

Implemented time-domain back-projection focusing from raw complex data; applied range compression, azimuth focusing, and multi-looking to produce high-resolution images with sharper visual quality than unfocused methods.

Methods & Tools

SAR Back-Projection Signal Proc Imaging
Outcome: Focused imagery of urban scenes with visual resolution improvement over unfocused baseline.
Orbit Determination

JahSat-1: EKF/UKF Orbit Determination

Performed OD with range/range rate from three stations (Kwajalein, Diego Garcia, Arecibo). Compared EKF vs UKF with residual analysis, RIC error evaluation, and scenario sweeps (range-only, rrate-only, per-station) over six days; executed 7th-day prediction.

Methods & Tools

EKF/UKF Residuals RIC Errors Bias Estimation
Outcome: Quantified filter performance by measurement mix & geometry; informed optimal tracking strategy.
EO Detection

EO Object Detection: Tree Crowns

Benchmarked Faster R-CNN / RetinaNet / SSD; integrated DeepForest baseline; prepared dataset protocol for fair comparison.

Methods & Tools

Detection CNN PyTorch
Outcome: Standardized pipeline to support future precision/recall benchmarking on EO imagery.
